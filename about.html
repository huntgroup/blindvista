About BlindVista

BlindVista is dedicated to making travel accessible, enjoyable, and empowering for people with disabilities. Founded on personal experience, our team understands the unique challenges travelers face and is committed to providing clear, accurate, and useful accessibility information for every journey.

Our Mission

To remove barriers, open up the world, and make travel easy and inclusive for everyone, regardless of ability.

Why Choose BlindVista?

  • We prioritise accessibility in everything we do.
  • Our team includes travellers with lived experience of disability.
  • We work with trusted partners worldwide.
