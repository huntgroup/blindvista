Accessibility Statement

BlindVista is committed to ensuring our website and services are accessible to everyone. We design and test our site to meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards, and welcome feedback on accessibility issues.

Accessibility Features

  • Clear, simple navigation
  • High contrast colours and large text
  • Keyboard and screen reader compatibility
  • Alt text for all images

Need help?

If you have any difficulty accessing our website or need information in a different format, please contact us.
