Accessible Travel for Everyone

Our Mission

At BlindVista, we believe that travel should be accessible to everyone, regardless of disability. Our mission is to provide inclusive travel options, guides, and bookings that remove barriers and make exploring the world easy, enjoyable, and safe for people with disabilities.

What We Do

We are building a platform that helps travelers with disabilities find the best destinations, accommodation, and travel services that meet their needs. From wheelchair-friendly hotels to guided tours designed for visually impaired travelers, BlindVista is here to make travel truly inclusive.
